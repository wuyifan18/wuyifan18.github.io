Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Peking University supervised by Prof. Ying Li. I received my B.E. in Software Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2019.

My research interests are AIOps and reliability management. In particular, my Ph.D. work focuses on anomaly detection and root cause analysis for change management in large-scale service systems.
